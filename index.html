Pedro Henrique Monteiro Torres

Pesquisador e Professor Adjunto

Sobre

Pesquisador e professor com vasta experiência em Microbiologia, Bioquímica e Biofísica, atuando principalmente nas áreas de biologia estrutural computacional, desenho de fármacos e bioinformática. Atualmente Professor Adjunto no Instituto de Biofísica Carlos Chagas Filho (UFRJ).

Pedro Henrique Monteiro Torres

Researcher and Associate Professor

About

Researcher and professor with extensive experience in Microbiology, Biochemistry and Biophysics, working mainly in the areas of computational structural biology, drug design and bioinformatics. Currently Associate Professor at the Carlos Chagas Filho Institute of Biophysics (UFRJ).

Awards and Titles

  • Young Scientist of Our State, FAPERJ (2023)
  • Vote of Commendation for the organization of the 3rd Biophysics Summer Course, IBCCF - UFRJ (2016)
  • Approval in Public Contest - Researcher in Public Health, Oswaldo Cruz Foundation (2014)
  • Approval in Public Contest - Substitute Professor, UERJ (2013)
  • Honorable Mention - São Paulo School of Advanced Science, EMBRAPA (2012)
